<h2 className="flex items-center gap-3"> <span className="flex items-center gap-3"><span className="icon-container yellow"><LightBulbIcon/></span></span> Questions for founders</h2>

Before we start, ask yourself:

**Do you have product-market fit or are you still validating?** If you're pre-validation, you might want something even leaner. If you've got traction, we'll build for scale.

**Will your team maintain the site or do you need ongoing support?** This determines whether Webflow or custom code makes more sense.

**What's your timeline to start taking signups?** If you need live in under 2 weeks, we can prioritize core pages and add supporting content later.

**Do you have brand assets (logo, colors) or are we starting from scratch?** We can work either way, but it affects timeline slightly.

**What's your competitor landscape like?** Crowded markets need stronger differentiation. Blue ocean products need more education.

**Do you have positioning and copy figured out?** We can work with what you have or help craft your positioning and final copy for the website.
